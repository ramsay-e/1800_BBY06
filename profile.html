Profile Settings

Q: Are my restriction and preference choices permanent?

A: No, you can change your restrictions and preferences with the "Edit diet restrictions/preferences" button above.

Q: How do I log out?

A: Below, there is a "Log Out" button that allows you to log out and access another account.

Q: Why do I have a mix of my restrictions and preferences?

A: We found that restrictions and preferences have large overlaps that would make multiple selections of the same category obsolete. For example, if you chose Vegan and Pescatarian, the Vegan concept would overide the other. In this case, multiple selections of restrictions and/or preferences displays and ensemble of all possible recipes.

Q: I have questions regarding the app and would like to speak to a representative, how can I do such?

A: Below, there is a "Contact Us" collapsible menu that displays the teams contact information. Feel free to send us an email!
